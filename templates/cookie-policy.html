This Cookie Policy explains how Identix uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website.

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide a better experience.

Cookies We Use

  • Session management (keeping you logged in)
  • Security features
  • Load balancing

Preference Cookies:

  • Theme selection (dark/light mode)
  • Language preferences
  • Display settings

Analytics Cookies:

  • Usage statistics
  • Performance monitoring
  • Error tracking

Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling cookies may affect functionality.
