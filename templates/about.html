Our Story

Identix was born from a capstone project focused on advancing facial landmark detection technology. What started as an academic pursuit quickly evolved into a comprehensive platform for facial analysis.

Our team recognized the growing need for accurate, real-time facial analysis tools that could be easily integrated into various applications - from security systems to healthcare diagnostics, from entertainment to accessibility solutions.

We built our platform on the foundation of BiSeNet, a cutting-edge semantic segmentation architecture, and enhanced it with custom training on extensive facial datasets. The result is a system that achieves industry-leading accuracy while maintaining real-time performance.

Today, Identix continues to evolve, adding new capabilities and refining existing ones to meet the ever-changing needs of our users and the technology landscape.
